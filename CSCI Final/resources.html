The Groundwater Hydrology Simulator is an ENGR-212 semester project carried out by Patrick Goosen, Joshua Tandoc and Tyler Verbeek to demonstrate the impact of both environmental and human effects on the flow of groundwater through stratified layers of sediment. The tank contains an upper layer of fine sand, a median layer of coarse sand, and a base of tight gravel separated from the sand by an impermeable layer of clay.
